\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents and manipulates Industrial I/O (IIO) paths that are used to uniquely identify IIO devices, attributes, and channels from a context. 

Examples of valid paths types\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item CONTEXT\+: "{}"{}
\item CONTEXT\+\_\+\+ATTR\+: "{}context-\/attribute"{}
\item DEVICE\+: "{}device-\/name"{}
\item DEVICE\+\_\+\+ATTR\+: "{}device-\/name/attribute-\/name"{}
\item CHANNEL\+: "{}device-\/name/chn-\/prefix\+\_\+channel-\/name"{}
\item CHANNEL\+\_\+\+ATTR\+: "{}device-\/name/chn-\/prefix\+\_\+channel-\/name/attribute-\/name"{} 
\end{DoxyItemize}
